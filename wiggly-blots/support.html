Wiggly Blots Support

Wiggly Blots is a puzzle-game with sketch-up design. Help wiggle blots to overcome barriers, dodge dangerous saw blades and change color. The goal of the game is to move naughty blots into a funnel. Hold and move your finger over the screen to bring them to their goal. Get rewards for perfect level completion.
